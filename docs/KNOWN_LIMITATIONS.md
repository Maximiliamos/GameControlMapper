# Known limitations

Version 1.0 is win-x64 only. Swipe is horizontal. Focus loss stops mapping without automatic restart. Mixed-DPI, multi-monitor and negative-origin environments remain `AutomatedOnly` until the corresponding guided scenarios pass on real hardware. XInput, Macro/Sequence runtime, Android/ADB, RawInput, Interception, ViGEm, pinch and rotation are unsupported. There is no installer, signature, auto-update, protection bypass or guaranteed compatibility with games and anti-cheat software.
